# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to a respectful and collaborative environment for all contributors.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on technical outcomes.
- Accept and give feedback professionally.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, abuse, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or intimidation.
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct.
